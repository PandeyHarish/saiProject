Sai Sumangal Radio Program

Sai Sumangal is a radio program broadcasted on radio stations across Nepal. Currently 39 radio stations are broadcasting the message of Bhagwan Sri Sathya Sai Baba. It is produced by the audio-visual unit of the SSSON. Sai Sumangnal was initiated as an offering at the divine lotus feet of Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba on the occasion of Swami's eighty-third birthday.

Sai Sumangal is based on a magazine format, with thirty minutes long production divided into various segments. These range from Bhajans to “Jigyasa - responses to questions received from listeners”, and from “Sai Avatar Gaatha - Bhagawan Sri SathyaSai Baba's Divine Life Story based on the text Sathyam Sivam Sundaram”, to narratives based on Bhagawan Baba's divine discourses.

The objective of Sri Sathya Sai Seva Organisation is to enable people to discover their innate divinity through selfless service.
